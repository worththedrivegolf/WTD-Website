The Story

Where It All Started

Worth the Drive Golf Studio started with a small winter league, a group of friends, and a simple realization — indoor golf can be a lot more than something to do when the weather turns.

It can be social. It can be competitive. It can be convenient. And for some golfers, it can also be private, focused, and available on their own schedule.

That is exactly what Worth the Drive is being built to deliver.

Worth the Drive On Tour brings the simulator experience to birthdays, company events, fundraisers, private parties, and local gatherings across Northeast Indiana. It is golf made more accessible — built for groups, events, and anyone who wants a fun way to play without needing a country club connection or a long drive to a big-city facility.

The Studio is the private side of that same vision. It is being built for golfers who want 24/7 member access, better practice tools, league opportunities, and a place to play without the pressure, noise, or distractions of a crowded bar.

Some golfers want the social side. Some want a quiet place to work on their game. Some want both.

Worth the Drive is being built for all of it — local golf, Midwest hospitality, and a better way to play close to home.

"Greatness isn't reserved for the gifted — it's earned by those who commit, stay consistent, and refuse to quit."
