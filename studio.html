🤖 Studio Technology

AI Swing Trainer Powered by ProTee VX

The Studio is being built around ProTee VX launch monitor technology — a ceiling-mounted, camera-based system designed to keep the hitting area clean while delivering detailed ball and club data. Members will be able to use real-time feedback, AI-assisted club tracking, swing video, shot recording, and performance data to practice with a purpose.

🏗️ Clean Overhead Setup

Ceiling-mounted tracking keeps the bay open with no floor unit in the hitting area — a cleaner, more natural experience at impact.

📊 AI-Assisted Club Data

Club path, face angle, club speed, impact location, and more help golfers understand what actually happened at impact — not just where the ball went.

🎥 Ball + Club Feedback

Dual high-speed cameras capture real-time ball and club data for more purposeful, informed practice sessions.

🔄 Lefty + Righty Friendly

Designed to support both right- and left-handed golfers without constantly reworking the bay setup.

📈 Practice With Proof

Swing camera support, shot recording, statistics, and visual feedback help turn practice into measurable progress over time.

🚫 No Special Equipment

No club stickers. No special golf balls. Bring your own clubs and balls — the system works with your existing gear.

Technology specifications are based on planned equipment and are subject to change prior to opening. The ProTee VX system is designed to support better practice — individual results will vary based on frequency and quality of use.
